The problem

Publishing is broken
in four places.

Modern publishers operate with rights in PDFs, localization across 10 vendors, and audience data in six disconnected dashboards. Inferiq fixes all of it.

P-01
📄
Rights are dark data
Contracts live in PDFs, spreadsheets, and lawyers' inboxes. No publisher knows in real-time what they can distribute, in which territory, on which platform — until it's too late.
P-02
🌐
Localization is a black hole
Translation, dubbing, and cultural adaptation are outsourced to 5–10 vendors with no central orchestration. Delays kill release windows and bleed margins.
P-03
📊
Audience data is siloed
Streaming, print, web, and social sit in disconnected dashboards. No unified view of who the audience is, what they want next, or where they're about to churn.
P-04
🤖
AI adoption is piecemeal
Publishers experiment with 10 point AI tools that don't talk to each other — creating new silos instead of solving old ones. Inferiq is the unified intelligence layer.
The platform

Three modules.
One operating system.

Each module solves a distinct problem. Together, they compound — creating a flywheel that gets smarter with every contract, every translation job, every audience signal.

📋
Rights Management
RightsCore™
AI that ingests contracts in any format and auto-extracts rights metadata — territory, medium, exclusivity windows, royalty triggers. Maintains a live Rights Ledger with expiry alerts and conflict detection.
  • Contract ingestion (PDF, DOCX, XML)
  • Live rights ledger & expiry alerts
  • One-click clearance reports
  • Multi-territory conflict detection
🌍
Content Localization
LocaleFlow™
End-to-end localization pipeline. AI draft translation → human-in-the-loop review → cultural adaptation scoring → vendor dispatch. 40+ languages. Tracks cost, turnaround, and quality.
  • AI draft + human review loop
  • Cultural adaptation scoring
  • Vendor dispatch & tracking
  • CMS & Adobe integration
📈
Audience Analytics
AudienceIQ™
Cross-platform audience intelligence. Ingests streaming telemetry, web analytics, social signals, CRM. Outputs segment-level engagement scores, churn propensity, and prescriptive recommendations.
  • Cross-platform data ingestion
  • Churn propensity scoring
  • Content affinity maps
  • Natural language query interface
🔗
AI Orchestration
Intelligence Layer
The connective tissue that makes Inferiq irreplaceable. RightsCore signals feed LocaleFlow. LocaleFlow performance data feeds AudienceIQ. AudienceIQ demand signals feed RightsCore. The whole is exponentially worth more than the parts.
  • Don't localize content you can't distribute — rights feed localization automatically
  • Discover which language markets convert — localization performance feeds audience models
  • Know which rights to acquire next — audience demand signals feed rights acquisition strategy
  • Domain-specific LLMs fine-tuned on media contracts and localization style guides
